Lemon Caper Pasta

Description

This zesty lemon caper pasta makes a beautiful side dish and pairs beautifully with poultry or white fish to make a simple yet elegant meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound rotini pasta, or any pasta
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon paste, (such as Better than BouillonÂ® Roasted Chicken Base)
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 tablespoons capers
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in pasta and return to a boil.
    Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
    Drain pasta and set aside.

  2. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add garlic; fry until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    Add remaining butter and melt, then sprinkle in flour. Whisk to combine; cook for about 1 minute.

  3. Whisk chicken soup base into reserved pasta water; pour into skillet. Whisk to make a smooth sauce. Whisk in lemon juice,
    1 tablespoon at a time, to taste. Stir in parsley and capers. Season with salt and pepper. Allow to simmer until sauce thickens, about 2 minutes.

  4. Toss sauce with drained pasta.
